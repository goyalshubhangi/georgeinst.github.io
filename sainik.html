Best Sainik School Coaching Institute in Dehradun

Best Sainik School Coaching in Dehradun

George Educational Institute (GEI) is one of the first coaching school established by Col. D. R. Patnaik, Dehradun in 1995. The school is situated at Nimbuwala Garhi Cantt, Dehradun, Uttarakhand. The primary aim of the school is to prepare students academically, mentally and physically for entry into the NDA, CDS, RIMC, ARMY/NAVY/Airforce. It enables students from economically weaker sections of the society to take up an illustrious officers carrier in the Armed Forces of the nation. The main qualities of this coaching institute in Dehradun are dedication, character building, patriotic outlook and esprit-de-corps for the students.
